An Alternative Architecture for AI

For centuries, publishers, authors, journalists, and educators have created the raw material for how societies learn and grow. But today, that knowledge is being quietly absorbed into AI systems — where lack of attribution and compensation creates a deep concern about the future of our information economy.

We are exploring alternative architectures to make AI inclusive and fair, with significant incentives for both AI providers and data owners.

Attribution-Based Control for Publishers

We're building attribution-based control where you decide the terms on how your content is used (and if it is used at all) in AI systems, whereas AI users - readers - can choose which sources they want to use when they ask a question.

Your Content on all AI surfaces

Through MCP connectors, end users' can explore using your content in their AI prediction in ChatGPT, Claude, etc with ease.

Direct Revenue from AI

Set your own pricing per query and earn immediately every time your content powers an AI response.

Brand Attribution

Every AI response ships with the sources, driving traffic and recognition back to your platform.

Audience Relationship Protection

Maintain direct relationships with your audience while expanding your reach through AI. No middleman, no data loss.

Why This Matters Now

In every information revolution, the rules are written early. Right now, companies are racing to define how AI systems access and distribute knowledge. If they succeed first, we risk building a future where a handful of players control the plumbing of intelligence.

The Web Revolution

We use HTTP and TCP/IP because they were there first

The Mobile Revolution

We live with closed platforms because no open alternative was ready in time

The AI Revolution

Now: Together, we can help define an open and pluralistic foundation: one where publishers stay visible and valued, AI systems become more accurate and trustworthy, and knowledge remains a shared resource.
