Fair AI Economy for Publishers

For generations, publishers, journalists, and educators have shaped how societies learn. Now, their knowledge is being absorbed into AI systems without credit or compensation - while users lose trust.

We're building an open, fair architecture that reconnects those who share knowledge with those who rely on it, with incentives for both AI providers and content owners.

How It Works: Attribution-Based Control (ABC)

We’re developing solutions that let publishers define how AI can use their content — while users decide which trusted sources power their answers.

Your Content on Every AI Surface

Appear across AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, and others — always with attribution and traceable usage.

Direct Revenue from AI

Earn instantly when your content powers an AI response. Set your own pricing per query or for each AI provider.

Protect Your Audience Relationship

No intermediaries. Keep ownership, traffic, and data — while extending your reach through AI.

Empower Collectives

Join or form collectives of publishers and creators to strengthen your negotiating power, share infrastructure, and establish collective legitimacy in AI ecosystems.

Why This Matters Now

In every information revolution, the rules are written early.
Today, private companies are racing to define how AI systems access and distribute knowledge. If they succeed, we risk a future where a few platforms control pricing, availability, and the very ownership of data.

The Web Revolution

We use HTTP and TCP/IP because open standards came first and shaped the internet we know.

The Mobile And Social Media Revolution

We live with closed ecosystems because they filled the gap, giving a handful of companies control over distribution, monetization, and audience relationships.

The AI Revolution

Now: Together, we can help define an open and pluralistic foundation: one where publishers stay visible and valued, AI systems become more accurate and trustworthy, and knowledge remains a shared resource.
