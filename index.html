The protocol for Network Sourced AI

Most of the world's intelligence is locked away—in private datasets, proprietary models, local envs, and individual expertise. Centralizing it isn't just hard — it doesn't scale. Intelligence is inherently distributed, and thus, developing AI must be collaborative.


This open-source protocol makes it simple to combine all of these models into powerful, permissioned ensembles.

It respects ownership, rewards contributions, and enables scale without sacrificing control.
